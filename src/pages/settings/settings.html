About

FCAD Connect is a new community hub for the Faculty of Communication and Design at Ryerson University. The app currently serves as a board for faculty news and events, and also provides notifications to users. In the near future, the app will include subscriptions to different types of content, community engagement & user submitted content, and notification settings.

This app is developed by FCAD OTI using the cross-platform Ionic framework.
